Selected fellow in a multi-year program focused on equity-minded teaching practices in STEM. Developed and implemented a pedaggy course for bioengineering graduate students, BioE 296. The weekly seminar explored best practices through guided discussions and workshops on effective and equitable pedagogy. Emphasis was on building practical skills for defining and accomplishing course objectives. Participants were able to implement these actionable inclusive teaching strategies to foster a community of belonging and equity within the classroom. Activities also built personal and professional skills useful for diverse future careers.
